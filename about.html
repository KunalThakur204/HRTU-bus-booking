About HRTU Transport

Founded in 1995, HRTU Transport has been the lifeline for students and faculty, ensuring safe, reliable, and comfortable inter-campus travel. With a fleet of modern buses and well-established routes, we provide seamless connections between the university and nearby stations, making travel hassle-free.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer affordable and timely transportation for all members of the Haryana Technical University community. We are committed to making every journey safe, efficient, and comfortable.

Our Fleet

Our fleet is made up of state-of-the-art buses, equipped with the latest safety features, comfortable seating, and air-conditioning for a pleasant ride. All buses are regularly maintained to ensure optimal performance and safety for our passengers.

Reach & Coverage

HRTU Transport connects various campuses, stations, and local points of interest across Haryana. We also offer services to neighboring states to ensure students and faculty can travel conveniently for academic and personal purposes.

History of HRTU Transport

Over the years, HRTU Transport has grown from a small fleet of buses serving the local campus community to a comprehensive and well-established transport network. Starting with just a few routes in 1995, we have expanded to cover over 50 routes, with hundreds of daily commuters relying on our services.

Our Core Values

  • Safety: The well-being of our passengers is our top priority.
  • Reliability: We pride ourselves on being punctual and dependable.
  • Comfort: We ensure that every journey is pleasant and enjoyable.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to eco-friendly transport solutions.
